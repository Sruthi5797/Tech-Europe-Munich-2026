Global Prevalence

A Unified Vision for Liver Care

By connecting all parties into a unified state, LiverLink simplifies chronic healthcare. Changes in one portal flow immediately to the next. When the **Lab** uploads blood work, the **Doctor** is instantly notified of abnormal ALT/AST levels, adjusting the patient's prescription. The **Patient** receives the revised dosage on their phone app, and their **Caregiver** gets an automated update ensuring compliance.
